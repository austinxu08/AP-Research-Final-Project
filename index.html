The
Feed

Social media platforms do not require your deliberate choice to radicalize you. They only require your attention.

Research on affective polarization shows that algorithmic content curation — optimized for engagement, not truth — systematically nudges users toward more emotionally intense, politically extreme content, regardless of intent. This effect is strongest among young users, and accelerates across all demographics.

This simulation replicates that logic. You will scroll a feed. You will engage or ignore. The algorithm will learn and adapt. At the end, you'll receive a data receipt — a mirror of what just happened to you.

You did not choose this. That's the point.

⚠ This simulation contains escalating political and social content designed to mirror real algorithmic radicalization. Posts are fictional but reflect real discourse patterns.
